Example stereograms

Here are some stereograms I've made using this tool. They're presented in a little widget which might help you see them.

If you click the stereogram itself, it will fullscreen the widget. If you find it easier to cross your eyes than uncross them then tick the "invert" checkbox. If you're having trouble uncrossing far enough, try reducing the "pattern width" slider. The ideal position for it depends on you and how big your screen is â€” generally on a larger screen you should reduce the slider. The "3D" slider on the right works the same as on the Nintendo 3DS. Lastly, the "cheat" checkbox reveals the hidden picture so you know what you're looking for.
